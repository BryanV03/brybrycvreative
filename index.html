03 — About

The Designer

Rooted in culture,
built through design.

I'm a graphic designer based in the Bay Area with a focus on brand identity, music editorial, and culture-driven visual work. My aesthetic is shaped by Mexican and Latin culture — from regional music to rancho tradition.

I founded Estilo Libre as a full brand project to push my work beyond the screen into real product — handling everything from the initial concept to apparel design, lookbook photography, and e-commerce.

Currently available for freelance projects, brand collaborations, and design work.
